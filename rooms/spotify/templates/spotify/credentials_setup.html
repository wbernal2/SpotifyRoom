Spotify Credentials Setup

How to Set Up Your Spotify App

Step 1: Create a Spotify Developer Account

Go to Spotify Developer Dashboard and log in with your Spotify account.

Step 2: Create a New App

Click "Create An App" and fill in the required information.

Step 3: Set Up Redirect URIs

In your app settings, add the following Redirect URI:

http://127.0.0.1:8000/spotify/redirect

Click "Add" and then "Save".

Step 4: Get Your Credentials

Copy your Client ID and Client Secret from the app dashboard.
